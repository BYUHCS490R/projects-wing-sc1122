We have different public transport that can travel to anywhere easily

Bus

A primary mode of public transport, featuring large, double-decker franchised buses

Minibus

A popular and efficient form of public transport that complement the MTR system. They are smaller than standard buses and can carry up to 19 passengers.

Train(MTR)

MTR (Mass Transit Railway)is Hong Kong's efficient and extensive public transport system, featuring multiple lines that cover the city. It is known for its speed, reliability, and integration with urban development, which includes numerous stations with shops and direct connections to residential and commercial properties.

Tram

Operating since 1904, it runs on a 30-kilometer track along the northern coast of the island and is famous for its green, double-decker cars, a symbol of the city's heritage and culture. The trams are often called "Ding Ding" due to the sound the bell makes.

Taxi

A key public transport service, distinguished by their colors: red for urban, green for the New Territories, and blue for Lantau Island. The passenger experience often involves fast driving, and riders are expected to pay cash only. Many are made by Toyota, and some recent additions aim to improve quality, such as wheelchair-accessible and hybrid models.
