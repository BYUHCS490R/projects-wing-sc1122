Here are some food that you should give it a try!

Wonton Noodles

A Cantonese noodle soup dish featuring thin, springy egg noodles in a flavorful broth, topped with translucent wonton dumplings filled with shrimp and pork.

Four Treasures Rice

Hong Kong's "Four Treasure Rice" typically features four types of meat or other ingredients served over rice, with variations depending on the restaurant. Common "treasures" include roasted meats like BBQ pork, roasted duck, and roast pork, along with Chinese sausage, a salted egg.

Egg Waffle

An egg waffle is a spherical egg-based waffle popular in Hong Kong, consisting of an eggy leavened batter cooked between two plates of semi-spherical cells.

Curry Fishball

Made with premium quality fish meat (over 50% fish content), these bite-sized treats capture the essence of traditional Hong Kong cuisine. Each ball is perfectly seasoned with a delightful curry flavor that delivers a satisfying kick to your taste buds.
