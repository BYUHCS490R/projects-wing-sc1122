Information:

Tws stand for Twenty four seven With Us, means they will be with their fans twenty four hours every day, a concept of boyhood pop. They was debuted on 22 January, 2024. Their signature is blue and their fans' name is Sai which means relationship in Korean.

There are six member in this male k-pop group. Five from Korea and one from China. Their age range is eighteen years old to twenty two years old. They are all very talented on singing and dancing, but the most talented on vocal is Youngjea, and Jihoon is the main dancer of this group. At last, Dohoon was chosen to be the center of the group since his facial appearance.
